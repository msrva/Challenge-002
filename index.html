My Past

I was born and raised in Richmond, VA. In 2013 I received a BFA in painting and printmaking from Virginia Commonwealth University. While in school, I dabbled in a language called Processing, which I used to make digital, interactive art projects embedded in web pages.

Present Day

I currently work as a tier 1 product support specialist for an Ed Tech company. The opportunity to contribute to projects outside of my primary function has kick-started my interest in web development.

The Future

I want to join my passion for art and design with coding skills to further explore my artistic ambitions and level-up my professional career.
